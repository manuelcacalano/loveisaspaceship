BIO

Manuel Cacalano is an argentinian artist, composer, producer and sound designer born in the city of Mar del Plata. love is a spaceship is a project that comes from the desire to escape even for a moment from reality using space-themed songs to explore grief, hope and ultimately, love. In 2019 he released "headlights" and "half asleep". In 2020 he teased the 3rd album to complete the trilogy with a self titled EP "love is a spaceship", a much more synthy based experience. Finally, on december 2020 love is a spaceship released "supernova", the final album on the grief, hope and love trilogy. He is currently working on a 4th album, far away from the themes that he already aboarded.
